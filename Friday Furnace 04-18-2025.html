TechTarget, Inc. (TTGT) ‚Äî TechTarget, Inc. announced that its Audit Committee and independent auditors determined the previously issued financial statements for the Informa Tech Digital Businesses covering 2022, 2023, and interim periods in 2024 contain material accounting errors related to goodwill impairment, contingent consideration, and intangible amortization, and are no longer relied upon. Global Clean Energy Holdings, Inc. (GCEH) ‚Äî Global Clean Energy Holdings, Inc. has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy and will operate as a debtor-in-possession under Bankruptcy Court supervision while implementing a pre-arranged reorganization plan. The company secured $250 million in debtor-in-possession (DIP) financing from DIP Lenders, including a revolving credit facility, term loan, and EPC agreement, to fund operations, pay prepetition obligations, and cover restructuring costs.
